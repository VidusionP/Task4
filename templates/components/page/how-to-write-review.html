digital media partner
Anyone can submit a review, but that wouldn't be authentic on our part nor the customer’s. That’s why we only value verified reviews from customers who have bought our products. To submit a verified review which qualifies for our Digital Media Partner program, please follow the steps below:
01
Look out for an email requesting you to review your purchased products around two weeks after your order has been received.
***Note: If you haven’t received this email by that time, please contact our customer support team.
02
Provide your rating for the product from one to five stars.
03
Name the title of your review (it can be a short summary of your review).
04
Provide a more detailed review of your product and experience using it.
05
Select “Yes” or “No” for the “Do you recommend this product?” option.
06
Leave the box unchecked so that your review is not anonymous.
***Note: As part of our Digital Media Partner program, you agree that we can identify you as a customer or client.
07
Click “Publish your review.”
08
You will then be redirected to a new form where you can upload photos or a video. Click on “Upload a Photo” or “Upload a Video” to add them to your review.
***Note: Remember to follow this step in order to gain benefits from our Digital Media Partner program.
09
You’re done! You can now share your review if you wish to do so.
