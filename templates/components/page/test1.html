How to select human hair toppers for women?

  1. The first step is to determine your hair loss stage as this will help you decide your hair topper size.
  2. Measure the affected hair loss (balding) area from front-to-back as well as side-to-side.
  3. Choose a base material (like monofilament) for your hair topper that is most suitable for your needs.
  4. Decide on the type of hair (like human hair) you want, the length of the hair as well as the color.
