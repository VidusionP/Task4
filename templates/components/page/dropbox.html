Overview

All you have to do is submit a video in addition to photos of you before and after you put on a Superhairpieces product whether it's a wig, toupee, topper, hair extensions or even eyelash extensions!

We will then upload these videos on the official Superhairpieces YouTube channel and whoever gets the most engagement points by the contest deadline will receive a grand prize of a US$1,000 Amazon gift card.

-- You will receive $50 in store credit just for submitting a video AND before/after photos

-- You will receive $100 in store credit for submitting a video of the step-by-step process of you installing the product AND before/after photos

Eligibility

To participate, the Participant (“You”) must be an existing or new Superhairpieces customer and have purchased our product(s).

The video contest is open to residents worldwide. Participants must be 18 years of age as of their date of entry in this contest in order to qualify.

How to Enter

    • Fill out your details in the contest at the bottom so we can link your video with your account details when you submit it.
    • Upload your video(s) and photos to the designated Dropbox link which we will provide along with the photos. See Photo and Video Requirements.
    • If everything fits the requirements, we will upload your video on our YouTube channel within a few days of your submission and apply the store credit to your Superhairpieces account!
  • Note: The earlier you submit your video, the more chances you will have of getting engagement points!

 

Photo and Video Requirements

To get $50 store credit

Submit a video AND before/after photos

  • Photo requirements
      • The photo's shorter side shouldn't be smaller than 1200 px and show the before/after hair from front, back, and top.
      • Format: JPG, JPEG
  • Video requirements
    • The video should be landscape and can either be about: 1) a quick before and after video 2) a short review about the hair system/extension, or 3) you rocking your new hair while doing different activities
    • Format: MP4, MOV, MPEG4, AVI, WMV, MPEG
    • Size: at least 1920×1080 pixels.
    • The minimum duration of the video is 60 seconds
  • Your face should be visible in both the video and photos. (If the participant is a professional or salon, the person or client in the photos and video should have their face visible)

To get $100 store credit

Submit a video AND before/after photos

  • Photo requirements
      • The photo's shorter side shouldn't be smaller than 1200 px and show the before/after hair from front, back, and top.
      • Format: JPG, JPEG
  • Video requirements
    • The video should be in landscape and shows step by step process of installing the hair system/extensions from beginning to end
    • Format: MP4, MOV, MPEG4, AVI, WMV, MPEG
    • Size: at least 1920×1080 pixels.
    • The minimum duration of the video is 2 minutes
  • Your face should be visible in both the video and photos. (If the participant is a professional or salon, the person or client in the photos and video should have their face visible)
