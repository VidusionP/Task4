Dealing with hair loss, balding or thinning for the first time?
You might be dealing with pattern baldness or alopecia. However, there could be many reasons for hair loss including stress or an infection.
Determine the cause of your hair loss by visiting your doctor for a check-up first.
The main causes
Men/Women
  • Genetics
  • Styling stress such as tight hairstyles, pulling the hair back, overdyeing hair
  • General stress which can cause telogen effluvium
Women
  • Temporary situations such as diet, stress, illness, medication, pregnancy and menopause
Determine the cause of your hair loss by visiting your doctor for a check-up first.
