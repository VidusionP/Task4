Roadmap

Step 1 Book a consultation
The first step is to book an online consultation with us.
This gives you the chance to speak to one of our experts and determine what hair system is best for you depending on your hair loss stage, needs and preferences. See our consultation FAQs
Step 2 Order your first hair system
Once our experts recommend the hair system that is best suited for your needs, it is time to place an order. You can do this on your own or have a Superhairpieces customer support agent place it for you for a small handling fee.
Step 3 Install your hair system
You can learn how to install it yourself. However as a first-timer, we recommend getting it installed by a professional.
Step 4 Learn how to take care & Maintain your hair system
We will give you tips and provide you with resources so you know how best to take care and maintain your hairpieces so that you get the most out of your new purchase.
