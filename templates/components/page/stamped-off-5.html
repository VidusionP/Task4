Why should you come see Patti?
Patti Wood, CT (Certified Trichologist), owner of Off 5th Avenue Salon in Bradenton, is a professionally trained hair stylist and a certified Trichologist specializing in Hair Replacement Therapy and solutions. Patti has helped many clients solve hair loss problems ranging from cancer treatments, male or female pattern baldness, hair thinning, and Alopecia Areata. She also works closely with dermatologists and physicians in treatment plans. Patti is now on the Board of Directors and an Educator for the prestigious US Trichology Institute (USTI).
Patti has over 35 years of experience as an educator and trainer in the hair industry. Working as a Regional Performing Artist for the Redken Corporation, Patti has been a guest speaker at many industry and non-industry events. She has also been a salon owner since 1982, managing staff from 8 up to 15 stylists and support staff.
Patti combines her extensive experience in hair care with her educational background to provide you quality service and insightful solutions to your hair care needs. Her training and her knowledge of hair helps her to understand what will work best for your individual situation. If hair re-growth isn't an option, Patti is an expert stylist and can find a natural hair piece that fits to you. Most hair pieces are fitted to your scalp and last about two weeks. The hair is natural and is cut, colored and styled to your personal preference. She is a committed hair care professional with a gift to share.
